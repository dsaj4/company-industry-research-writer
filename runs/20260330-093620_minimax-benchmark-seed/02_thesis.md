# Thesis

## Central judgment

- The MINIMAX report is a strong benchmark for structure, evidence density, and chapter rhythm in AI company deep-dive research, but it must be abstracted carefully because its stock-rating and listed-company valuation machinery do not transfer cleanly to every target.

## Why this is useful to the skill

- It shows how a complete institutional report turns model capability, product progress, and commercialization signals into a coherent investment thesis.
- It also highlights the exact boundaries the skill must enforce when adapting listed-company research patterns to private-company or non-rating outputs.

## Chapter question tree

1. How does the report establish the company identity and stage?
2. How does it justify that the sector matters now?
3. How does it isolate the company's most important differentiators?
4. How does it convert qualitative strengths into economic or valuation language?
5. How does it define risks that can invalidate the thesis?

## Highest-priority evidence by chapter

- Company overview:
  - timeline
  - management table
  - product matrix
  - pricing screenshots
- Industry analysis:
  - technology progress charts
  - market-size charts
  - peer and benchmark tables
- Company highlights:
  - model rankings
  - usage and revenue split charts
  - internationalization data
- Valuation:
  - business forecast table
  - comparable-company valuation table
- Risks:
  - explicit downside cases tied to execution and competition
