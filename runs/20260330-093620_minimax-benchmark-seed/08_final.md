# Final

## Final benchmark judgment

The GF Securities MINIMAX report is a high-value benchmark sample for company and industry research skill-building because it combines:

- a disciplined institutional report skeleton
- heavy evidence planning through figures and tables
- a clear capability -> product -> commercialization -> valuation chain

## Final usage rule

Use it to learn:

- report structure
- chapter rhythm
- evidence density
- figure and table responsibilities

Do not use it to inherit:

- rating language
- target-value logic
- listed-company financial-statement machinery
- MiniMax-specific business splits
