# Draft

## Benchmark abstraction draft

This sample should be treated as a structural benchmark rather than a reusable valuation template.

Its strongest reusable features are:

- front-loaded core conclusions
- stable chapter architecture
- aggressive use of figure and table planning
- explicit bridge from capability to product to economics

Its least reusable features are:

- rating language
- target-value logic
- listed-company forecast machinery
- MiniMax-specific business decomposition
