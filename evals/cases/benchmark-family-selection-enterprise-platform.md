# Benchmark Family Selection: Enterprise Platform Case

## Task type

- benchmark-family selection
- calibration note generation

## Target object

- A private enterprise AI company focused on workflow automation, internal knowledge orchestration, and decision support for large industrial and financial customers

## Company type

- private company

## Audience

- internal research team

## Desired deliverable

- benchmark choice
- primary/secondary borrowing logic
- calibration note

## Available high-level signals

- the company's main strength is not a consumer-facing AI product
- it sells platform capabilities and solution architecture into enterprises
- customer strategy, deployment depth, and industry penetration matter more than broad consumer usage metrics
- management often frames the company around workflow integration and decision quality rather than model leaderboard rank
- commercialization quality is uneven across customers, so challenge and constraint analysis may matter

## What this case is testing

The output should show that the skill can:

1. choose the enterprise-platform benchmark as primary
2. explain why the model-and-product-led benchmark is not the best primary fit
3. optionally borrow one narrow pattern from the model-led benchmark without collapsing both benchmarks into one structure
4. explicitly state what listed-equity elements must not transfer

## What good output should contain

- chosen primary benchmark
- rationale tied to archetype
- optional secondary pattern borrowed, if any
- transfer boundaries
- a calibration note suitable for `01_calibration.md`
