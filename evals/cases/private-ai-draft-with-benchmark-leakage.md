The company has already formed a very clear business split, with consumer AI products, enterprise services, and overseas revenue all entering a stable growth phase. Similar to the benchmark sample, the company overview, valuation, and risk profile are already sufficient to justify a direct target-value framework.

Moonshot's market cap remains attractive relative to its revenue, and the company should continue to see audited revenue growth as Kimi gains users and API demand expands. The latest target valuation also confirms the completed valuation of the business.

In our view, the most important conclusion is that Moonshot has already completed the same commercialization path as the benchmark company, so the same valuation system can be applied with only minor adjustments.
